SAFETY AND SECURITY

As we move forward digitally, cybersecurity becomes more important. Technology provides advanced tools to protect personal data, prevent fraud, and ensure safer online transactions, giving people more confidence to use digital platforms.Our lives are stored online photos, bank details, and private messages. Without proper protection, this information can be misused. Governments around the world are now enforcing data privacy laws (like GDPR) to give people control over their information. On a personal level, users should avoid oversharing online and be careful with permissions given to apps.Social platforms are fun, but they also expose users to threats like identity theft, scams, and cyberbullying. Privacy settings, careful sharing, and reporting suspicious accounts are key to staying safe.

Hackers are becoming smarter, but so is technology. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is now being used to detect threats, block suspicious activities, and analyze huge amounts of data in real time. This means faster responses to attacks and stronger protection for users.
